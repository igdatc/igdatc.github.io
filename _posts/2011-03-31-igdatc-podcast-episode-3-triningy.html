IGDA Twin Cities Podcast, Episode 3 – Triningy

Local goings-on, IGDA’s recent elections, Last Month’s Meeting Recap and Upcoming Events, Game Engines for Indies

IGDA Twin Cities marches on!  Get it, its a pun!
